Find and read your story. From all of the stems and branches.

Just like the fact that only one branch on a one tree is nonsense, One ending in a one story is just a shabby stuff here. Every single story, event or character has tons of there own hidden stories. In this place, you will find infinte possibilitys on your most loved stories ever. You won't be dissapointed by just one, but not cool enough story line and it's ending. We sure all of storyholics on Middle-earth will love Branchers. we know that, because there will be no "The ends" on imagination of story lovers.
